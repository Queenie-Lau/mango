Green-Clean Your Clothes
Use biodegradable laundry detergents, hang your clothes out to dry when possible, and wait over one week to wash clothes.


Alter Your Driving Habits
During traffic hour when cars are at stand stills for long stretches of time, save fuel and reduce carbon dioxide emissions by turning off your car


Be Mindful of How You Spend Your Money
Often times, we buy more than what we need out of habit. By being more aware and planning out beforehand, you can create less waste by spending only on what you need.

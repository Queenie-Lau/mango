Inspiration
―

We developed a mobile app that inspires, recommends, and teaches you about the enviorment--like a close friend! Its primary function is a chatbot named Pudding, whose algorthim responds to any questions you ask and details tips on how to be enviromentally friendly for the day. It is perfect for people who don’t know where to begin in their sustainble journey, as Pudding is at your pocket when you need it. Our app allows users to scan their product and Pudding will output whether or not it is compostable. Moreover, it provides a map of recycling places and an inspiration board!
